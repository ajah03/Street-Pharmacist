Welcome to Street Pharmacist

Our app is designed to guide you through safe self-care using over-the-counter medication and holistic practices. It's a tool for those moments when professional medical help isn't immediately available.

Important: This app is not a replacement for professional medical advice. Please consult a healthcare provider whenever possible.

Features

Medication Scanner

Scan OTC medications to learn their uses and possible side effects.

Interaction Checker

Input your current medications to check for harmful interactions. Visit Drug Interaction Checker.

Holistic Guidance

Explore breathing exercises, tea remedies, and dietary suggestions tailored to your needs.

Ingredient Suggestions

Get recommendations for active ingredients to look for in OTC drugs. For example, dextromethorphan helps treat coughs.

Label Translation

Use our partner tool to translate medication labels: MediTranslate.

Suggested Translation Questions:
  • "How long does it take to work?"
  • "What are the side effects?"
  • "Will this make me sleepy?"
  • "How should I take this medicine?"

Emergency Help

Quickly contact 911 or locate nearby clinics in case of urgent needs.

About/FAQ

Is this app a replacement for doctors?

No, this app is a supplementary tool for safe self-care when immediate access to a healthcare provider is not possible.

How does the app ensure safety?

We provide disclaimers and direct users to professionals for serious concerns. Content is reviewed by healthcare professionals.

Contact & Emergency Resources

If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please call 911 immediately.

For non-emergencies, visit your nearest clinic or hospital.
